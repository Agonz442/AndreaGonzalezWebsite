Hello! I am Andrea Gonzalez and I currently work for Moreno Valley Unified School District. Working for the district has opened my eyes to the abundance of resources we have for all our students. My passion is helping others and any way I can I will! I currently hold the position as Guidance Assistant assisting our counselors which allows me to work with students and provide information pertaining to programs we offer as well as tutoring schedules. I am responsible for requesting records for newly enrolled students who are either coming from different schools within our District, or outside of our district. This allows us to schedule students in the appropriate classes.

I am responsible for scheduling 504 meetings between teachers and parents in order to devise a plan for students with disabilities in order for students to succeed with every opportunity available to them. Apart from 504 accomodation plans, I also assist with SST's which are Student Success Teams that meet with students, parents, and teachers to identify behavior problems as well as grades falling short. The team develops a plan with the student so we can get an idea of the why's and how's a student may not be motivated to work.

I hope to make my imprint on students for the better and always being a helping hand and listener to those who may feel their voice does not matter. I strive for the best professionally and personally! My goals for the future is to obtain my Masters in Counseling and to continue helping our youth become well rounded individuals. I take pride in my work and hope to also strive to be the best me I can be. When I was in my early years navagating through middle school and high School, it was always important to me knowing I had a helping hand in the adults around to steer me in the right direction. Just as I received guidance, I hope that students get the same with our staff members.
